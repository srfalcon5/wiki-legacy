## To light the stage
  - Channel 1: set to 8
  - Channel 2: set to 8
  - Channel 3: set to ~3.5
    - For people with darker skin, turn down from ~3.5 until glare on skin is minimal or gone while preserving the white of the columns behind
  
  **If needed,** also set:
    - Channel 4 to ~4.5
    - Channel 5 to 3

## To light the green screen
  - Channel 7 to 10
  - Channel 8 to 10
  - Channel 9 to 4
  
# To light the couch
  - Channel 10 to 9
  - Channel 11 to 9
  - Channel 12 to ~4.5
